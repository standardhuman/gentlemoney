Feeling Stressed About Your Finances?

When it’s time to look at your finances, do you feel a sense of mastery and calm? Or, like many of us, would you rather face anything else than your financial anxiety, distress, and shame? You're not alone, and there's a gentler way.

Inside Gentle Money

Drawing on his extensive experience supporting people through their most tangled financial challenges, Adam Koren illuminates the deep-seated reasons why finances are so hard in our culture. He offers a compassionate and practical approach to help you:

  • Understand why you avoid dealing with your finances so you can start doing it differently.
  • Navigate common financial hurdles like budgeting, saving, debt, taxes, and income.
  • Uncover the roots of your financial beliefs and understand how they impact you now.
  • Develop a gentle approach to money that fosters self-acceptance and confidence.

Whether you're struggling with debt, feeling lost in retirement planning, or just seeking a more peaceful relationship with your finances, Gentle Money provides the tools and insights you need to create lasting financial well-being. It’s a short, easy read that results in long-lasting changes.

Meet Adam Koren

Financial coach Adam Koren guides you on a journey to transform your relationship with money. With extensive experience supporting individuals through complex financial challenges, Adam brings a unique blend of practical wisdom and compassionate understanding to the often-stressful topic of personal finance.

His work focuses on illuminating the deep-seated reasons why finances can be so difficult, offering tools and insights to foster self-acceptance, confidence, and lasting financial well-being. You can learn more about Adam and his approach at adamkoren.com.
